Green Your Lab News


"What's up Green Your Lab?"

October 2021

We were interviewed by S&P Global for our take on how US Pharama giants are doing to combat the climate crisis. 

We also were invited by GoldBio to host a webinar on "10 sustainable things that every lab can start doing right now". We armed participants with ten practical actions they  can make to start their Green Lab journey off with impact. We give  some advice and recommendations for adopting sustainable practices that will save lab's money and improve the health and safety of the lab.


September 2021

This was an exceptionally busy month. We joined Great Big Green Week and hosted an "Interactive Webinar on Reducing your Carbon Footprint for Scientists". 

We also joining forces with NESSE (Network of Early Career Scientists and Engineers) and 500 Women Scientists and to host two Happy Hour Networking Events. 

August 2021

We've started our own club on Clubhouse! And we're doing our first session on Aug 6th. It just will just be a small personal chit chat about Green Labs and Sustainable Science. Oh, and we have free Clubhouse invitations to give away. 


July 2021

We had a great time as an Expert Panelist on a "Setting Up Your New Lab" webinar. We explored ways to start your lab on the right foot by thinking about sustainability upfront. We were joined by panelists from the University of Colorado, University of Delaware, University of California, Irvine, and Chowan University. 


June 2021

We're super excited to have been invited to speak at the ACS - 25th Annual Green Chemistry & Engineering Conference. The conference went from June 14-18th, 2021 with lots of talks from leading Green Chemistry experts. We spoke on the topic of Translating Green Lab Best Practices from Academia to Industry. We really enjoyed meeting everyone there. 


May 2021

We partnered with the Life Sciences Club to do our first Clubhouse session! We discussed Green Labs and Sustainable Science. 

We've started beta testing our new upgraded Green Your Lab community platform. We've got an awesome Green Lab Best Practices Database going. Want need you to help. Request an invitation here and give us your suggestions for improvement.

Congrats to Fanny, Holly and Shannon for pitching Green Your Lab at the TechStars Startup Weekend. Even though Green Your Lab is not technology focused, the judges could see the need and our potential. Thanks to all the mentors for their fantastic advice.


April 2021

In honor of Earth Week, Green Your Lab has been invited to talk at 500 Women Scientists. Join us on April 21th @ 9am PST. 

Earth.org published our article on Green Labs. We tackle the problem of why Green Labs is so challenging and offer some quick tips to get started. Read it here. 

Green Your Lab has been invited to e@UBC. We're excited to have their mentorship to maximize the impact of our community. 

We've started alpha testing our new upgraded Green Your Lab community platform. Keep watch, very exciting times ahead!


March 2021

Thank you to everyone for making our community great. We've received generous sponsorship for our upgrade. Look out for opporunities to alpha and beta test our new network platform.


Feb 2021

We are delighted to delivery a workshop on "Sustainability in the Lab, Pilot Plant and Life" to the students of Rochester University. 

Check out Little Da Vincis video podcast episode featuring Green Your Lab where we share tips that young scientists can use in the lab and at home.  


Jan 2021

Pharma Technology Focus Magazine interviewed Green Your Lab to discuss the Pharma Industry's sustainability goals. You can read the article in their Jan 2021 issue here. The full interview can be found in our Sustainability-Industry articles page. 

Green Your Lab has been invited to join California Senate Candidate, Sharifah Hardie's Round Table Talk Show. We will be live Live April 13th @ 8am PST. Join us.
